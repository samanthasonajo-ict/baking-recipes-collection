Our Sources & Inspirations

The Baking Recipes Collection was built with love — but also with help from many wonderful resources. This page serves as our way of saying thank you to the creators and bakers who inspire us every day. Every recipe, image, and word comes together because of the passion shared by the global baking community.

While most recipes were adapted and rewritten to make them simple and approachable, some inspiration came from classic cookbooks, online tutorials, and home bakers who generously share their creations. Images used for this project were sourced from free platforms like Pinterest and Pexels.

A special thanks to the baking enthusiasts, food bloggers, and teachers who continue to make baking knowledge accessible to everyone. This project is dedicated to them — and to you, the readers and bakers, who keep the joy of baking alive.
