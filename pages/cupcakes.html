Vanilla Cupcakes with Buttercream Frosting

Cupcakes are small, delightful cakes that are individually portioned and perfect for sharing. This recipe highlights the classic vanilla cupcake, which is fluffy, moist, and has a soft crumb that pairs beautifully with creamy buttercream frosting. Making cupcakes is simple: start by creaming together butter and sugar until light and fluffy, then add eggs and vanilla for flavor. Dry ingredients like flour and baking powder are folded in, alternating with milk to keep the batter smooth. Once baked, the cupcakes are cooled and topped with swirls of buttercream. You can also add sprinkles, edible glitter, or even fruit toppings to personalize them for parties or celebrations. Cupcakes are versatile, easy to serve, and guaranteed to brighten up any gathering with their fun appearance and sweet taste.
