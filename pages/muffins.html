Blueberry Muffins

Muffins are quick breads that can be sweet or savory, making them a versatile snack or breakfast option. This blueberry muffin recipe creates moist, fluffy muffins filled with bursts of juicy blueberries. The batter is made by whisking together flour, sugar, and baking powder, then adding in milk, eggs, and melted butter. The blueberries are gently folded into the batter to avoid breaking them, ensuring each bite has a sweet pop of flavor. A sprinkle of sugar on top before baking gives the muffins a golden crust and a slight crunch. Muffins are best enjoyed warm from the oven, but they can also be stored and reheated for a quick snack. You can substitute blueberries with chocolate chips, cranberries, or even nuts, making muffins a customizable treat for all occasions.
