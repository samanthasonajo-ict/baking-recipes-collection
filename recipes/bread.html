Homemade White Bread

Homemade bread is one of life’s simple pleasures. There’s nothing quite like the smell of freshly baked bread filling your kitchen. A classic white bread loaf only requires a few ingredients: flour, yeast, water, salt, and sugar. Knead the dough until smooth, allow it to rise until doubled in size, then bake at 375°F (190°C) for around 25–30 minutes.

The beauty of bread-making lies in its flexibility. You can experiment with whole wheat flour, oats, seeds, or even herbs for unique flavors. Beginners might find kneading a bit challenging, but it’s a skill that becomes easier with practice. Once you bake your first loaf successfully, you’ll find it hard to go back to store-bought bread. Homemade bread is healthier, fresher, and filled with love.
