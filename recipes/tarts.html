Fruit Tart

A fruit tart is a stunning dessert that combines a crisp pastry shell, creamy custard filling, and colorful fresh fruits. The tart shell is made from sweet pastry dough, baked until golden and crisp. Once cooled, it is filled with a silky custard made from milk, egg yolks, sugar, and vanilla. The custard is chilled to set, creating a smooth base for the fruit topping. Popular fruit choices include strawberries, kiwi, blueberries, mango, and peaches, arranged in beautiful patterns. To give the tart a glossy finish and keep the fruit fresh, a thin layer of apricot glaze is brushed on top. Fruit tarts are not only delicious but also visually stunning, making them the perfect centerpiece for parties, celebrations, and special occasions. They strike the perfect balance between sweet, creamy, and refreshing flavors.
